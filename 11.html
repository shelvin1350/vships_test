ACT Group
Gopal T Sujan
Owner ACT

ACT group is one the leading service providers in the Shipping & Logistics arena offering various services including Stevedoring, Warehousing, Transportation, Ship Agency, Customs Broking, Ship-broking.

The group was established in 1982 and now offers its services across various ports in India with a strong team of more than 400 employees.

Propel Shipping is a global dry-bulk shipping operator based out of Singapore with group offices in Dubai, Greece & India. The company annually carries over 20 million MT of cargo globally for it's various customers in the Steel, Cement, Power, Fertilizer & Mineral sectors.

Propel Shipping is the Chartering/Operating Arm of the ACT group in India.

ACT is owned by Gopal Sujan and has its headquarter in Kandla (India).

The company owns 10 bulk carrier ships, of which 6 managed by V Ships India and the remaining managed by Su-NAV, a in house JV company.

DG Shipping
Shyam Jagannathan

The Directorate General of Shipping (DGS), under the Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways, oversees India's merchant shipping. Established in 1949, it implements maritime policies, ensures sea safety, promotes maritime education, regulates seafarer welfare, and develops the shipping industry. The Director General exercises statutory powers under the Merchant Shipping Act, 1958.

Shri Shyam Jagannathan, a 1997 batch IAS officer of the Assam-Meghalaya cadre, is the current Director General of Shipping. With extensive experience in admin. and finance, he leads transformative initiatives to digitize and streamline DGS operations and reform examinations in line with STCW standards, driving innovation and governance excellence.
