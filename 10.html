M Pallonji Group

M Pallonji Group of Companies was established in 1927, primarily as a paint marketing company. Today, the companies of the M Pallonji Group have several entities engaged in diverse activities. These include painting, dredging, stevedoring, logistics solutions, shipping, finance, investments, life insurance, automobile dealerships and manufacture of specialty coatings.

The company is owned by two brothers, Mehli and Pheroze (our main contact) Mistry, of Parsi origins.

The two brothers are cousin of former Tata's Chairman Cyrus Mistry, younger brother of Shapoor, heir of the Shapoorji Pallonji Group.

M Pallonji owns 10 ships as follows:
- 4 PSVs Indian Flag in domestic trade
- 3 tankers (1 managed by V Ships India, 2 by Fleet)
- 3 bulk carriers (1 managed by V Ships India, 2 by BSM)

M Pallonji has recently acquired a Post Panamax bulk carrier that is expected to be awarded to V Ships India with takeover at the end of November.
