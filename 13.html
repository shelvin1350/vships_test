Significance of Rangoli and Lamp Lighting

  • Rangoli symbolizes a warm welcome, prosperity, and positivity. It reflects hospitality, creativity, and teamwork—expressing joy in welcoming our guest.
  • Lamp Lighting marks the beginning of an auspicious occasion. The flame signifies knowledge, wisdom, and the triumph of light over darkness—symbolizing clarity and collective progress.

Together, these traditions blend heritage with harmony, setting a tone of warmth, unity, and optimism as we welcome our esteemed guest.
