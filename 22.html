Shri Vijay Kumar (IAS)

Vijay Kumar, a 1992-batch IAS officer of the AGMUT cadre, serves as Secretary in the Ministry of Ports, Shipping, and Waterways. He holds a B.E. in Electronics, an M.B.A. from the University of Sydney, and completed the Micro Master program in Economics from MIT.


He has held numerous high-level positions in various departments, both in India and internationally. His experience spans key sectors, including education, trade, excise, water supply, and transportation.
